**Dr Florian Lüskow**
Dr. Florian Lüskow is a dedicated marine biologist and biological oceanographer with an extensive background in the ecology of gelatinous zooplankton. He has considerably contributed to the scientific community's understanding of the freshwater jellyfish _Craspedacusta sowerbii_, particularly through his highly collaborative work on the species’ global distribution and environmental drivers. Florian brings rigorous academic expertise to the UK team, focussing on mapping the spread of this non-native species and advancing our knowledge of its impact on freshwater ecosystems and societies.
